LifeShield Created for Accident Emergency purpose is a social road accident response web-based application. Our functionalities are: To provide real-time information on road accidents, to alert road users of accident-prone areas as well as danger-zones whilst on the road, to enable accidents to be reported to appropriate authorities and / or response teams, and to help authorities and / or respond teams respond to accidents and monitor occurrences.
